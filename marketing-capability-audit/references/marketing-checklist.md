# Marketing Capability Checklist (2026)

Use this as the scoring list for audits. Mark each item: Ready / Partial / Missing.

## Market + Customer
- Competitor list and monitoring
- ICP definition and segmentation
- Customer interviews or ongoing discovery
- Early access or waitlist pipeline

## Positioning + Messaging
- Value proposition and differentiation
- Messaging doc for homepage, ads, sales
- Proof points (case studies, testimonials)

## Website + Conversion
- Clear landing page and pricing page
- Primary CTA and conversion flow
- Performance and mobile checks
- Analytics baseline (events + goals)

## Email + Lifecycle
- Email capture flow
- Welcome / onboarding sequence
- Newsletter or update cadence
- Deliverability basics (SPF/DKIM/DMARC)

## Content + SEO
- Keyword research and content plan
- 3-5 core pages for top queries
- Lead magnet or gated asset (if relevant)
- Internal linking and technical SEO checks

## Social + Community
- Consistent brand handles
- 1-2 priority platforms with cadence
- Community or influencer list (if relevant)

## PR + Partnerships
- Media list and pitch angles
- Product directory or listing plan
- Partner shortlist and outreach plan

## Paid + Growth Experiments
- Landing page and tracking ready for paid
- Retargeting plan (if applicable)
- A/B testing plan for core pages

## Retention + Referral
- Activation checklist or in-app milestones
- Churn / retention metrics tracked
- Referral loop or customer advocacy plan

## Notes on Items That Require Updates
- HARO: still viable as “press source” but tool/provider may change; validate the current platform and rules.
- Tag Assistant: still needed for tag verification; use the current Google Tag Assistant extension and debugging flow.
